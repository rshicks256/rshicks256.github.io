Games

I have been a gamer for as long as I can remember. I grew up playing video games with my twin brother on the N64 and the Gameboy. My favorite games at that time included The Legend of Zelda (N64), Star Fox (N64), Super Smash Brothers (N64), and Pokemon Red & Blue (Gameboy). My brother and I teamed up using game link cables and eventually managed to collect all 150 Pokemon--which I still, comically, claim to be my greatest accomplishment to this day. I also grew up playing card games such as Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh, and Magic: The Gathering. As I grew older, I began playing mainly console and PC games such as The Elder Scrolls (XBOX-RPG), Warcraft III (PC-RTS), Dota/HoN (PC-MOBA), and Halo (XBOX-FPS). Nowadays, you will probably find me PvPing in the World of Warcraft (PC-MMORPG) or serving out headshots in either PUBG (PC-3PS/FPS) or COD: Black Ops 4 (XBOX-FPS). Below you will find a gallery of my World of Warcraft characters. I love transmogrification! If you ever want to play together, please feel free to join my discord community by visiting the link at the bottom of the page.
