Games

I have been a gamer for as long as I can remember. I grew up playing video games with my twin brother on the N64 and the Gameboy. My favorite games at that time included The Legend of Zelda (N64), Star Fox (N64), Super Smash Brothers (N64), and Pokemon Red & Blue (Gameboy). My brother and I teamed up and eventually managed to collect all 150 Pokemon, which I still claim as my greatest accomplishment to this day. I also grew up playing card games such as Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh, and Magic: The Gathering. As I grew older, I began playing more console and PC games such as The Elder Scrolls (XBOX-RPG), Warcraft III (PC-RTS), Dota/HoN (PC-MOBA), and Halo (XBOX-FPS). Nowadays, you will probably find me PvPing in the World of Warcraft (PC-MMORPG) or sniping noobs in either PUBG (PC-3PS/FPS) or COD: Black Ops 4 (XBOX-FPS).
