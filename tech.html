Technology

Nothing excites me more than technology. My current technology interests include: space exploration, robotics, machine learning, programming, biotechnology, renewable energy, and cyber security. I have a grand idea of a universal being that is able to colonize our entire universe. Despite this vested interest, I am also a spiritual, humanitarian being and have come to the realization that this intergalactic conquest may not be in the best interest of neither the Earth nor the entire intergalactic confederation. For this reason, I am unsure where to continue my research. On one hand, I want to improve this world by accelerating our understanding of science and technology. On the other hand, I want the human race--and any other current forms of extraterrestrial life--to continue to thrive. I would love to hear any of your opinions on this subject matter!
